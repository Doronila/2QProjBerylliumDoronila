Plot: The universe of JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a reflection of the real world but with supernatural forces and beings.

The narrative is split into different parts with independent stories and different characters.

Each protagonist is a member of the Joestar Family.


Original Run: October 6, 2012 – present

About Manga: Written and illustrated by Hirohiko Araki, the Jojo's Bizarre Adventure manga began its serialization in Weekly Shōnen Jump's combined issue #1-2 of 1987 on January 1, 1987.
