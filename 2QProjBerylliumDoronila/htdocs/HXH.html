Plot: The story follows a young boy named Gon Freecss, who was told all his life that both his parents were dead.

But when he learns from Kite, an apprentice of his father Ging Freecss, that he is still alive and has since become an accomplished Hunter,

Gon leaves his home on Whale Island to take the Hunter Examination in order to become a Hunter like him.


Original Run: October 2, 2011 – September 24, 2014

About Manga: Written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi, the Hunter × Hunter manga began its serialization in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine on March 16, 1998.

The first volume was released on June 4, 1998, as of October 4, 2018, thirty-six volumes have been released.
